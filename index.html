About Me

I am currently working at Yelp as a Product Manager in San Francisco, California, I have a Computer Science and Business background from University of Waterloo and Wilfrid Laurier University respectively. In my spare time, I like to play guitar, go on photo walks, and discover nature. I am passionate about programming, design, and all things tech.

Design

I love designing simplistic, clean UI with a focus on ease of useability and I try to pay attention to designs of websites, software, and ordinary everyday objects to understand why they are designed the way they are.

Development

Programming is my passion, solving interesting problems with even more interesting solutions is what I do in my spare time. I love applying theoretical computer science concepts and ideas and modelling them in real life.

Teamwork

I love working with people and working together to solve a complex problem. Being able to turn an idea into a finished product and having a hand in all the areas of shipping a product is one of the most interesting and rewarding things I can do.
